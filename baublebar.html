BaubleBar

Online Jewelry Retailer

Founded in 2011 to serve the fashion jewelry market, which the company founders had identified as lacking a dedicated retailer that offered quality products at an affordable price point.

BaubleBar has been called the “future of retail” by Mashable. Its products have garnered media attention from fashion magazines such as Glamour, People StyleWatch, Cosmopolitan and others. The co-founders were featured in "30 under 30: America’s Coolest Young Entrepreneurs of 2012" by Inc. Magazine. In addition to a collaboration with Anthropologie in January 2014, Nordstrom and BaubleBar announced a partnership where BaubleBar's products will be also be sold in 35 of Nordstrom's top US retail locations in March 2014.

Headquarters are located in New York, New York.
